Only ICC judges can determine Duterte's interim release — spokesman

MANILA, Philippines — Only the International Criminal Court (ICC) can decide on the interim release of former president Rodrigo Duterte, a spokesman for the global tribunal said on Wednesday. Fadi El Abdallah issued the statement in response to petitions regarding the legality of Duterte's arrest, which had been submitted to the Philippine Supreme Court. He said that only ICC judges could approve a suspect's release, and any request must go to them for evaluation. El Abdallah declined to comment on the Philippines' legal processes, emphasizing that the country was responsible for deciding on how to handle these issues.
